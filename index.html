Between deadlines, hot fixes, office politics, irrelevant meetings, and endless caffeine and fast food, the stress and habits brought about by work may have you on a path headed for serious health problems. Training — Let Someone Else Do the Programming

DevLifts Basic

Our in-house personal trainer has worked with developers, identified their common strengths and weaknesses, and designed specified training programs geared towards correcting the muscular imbalances that come with a sedentary lifestyle and relieving the bodily stress faced by developers. With DevLifts Basic, you'll have access to these basic programs in addition to helpful articles for reworking your fitness.

DevLifts Pro

Our premium packages allows one-on-one access to a personal trainer who will work specifically with you to create a workout plan that fits who you are -- including your likes, dislikes, goals, body type, and lifestyle. Your trainer will be as involved or hands-off as you like, and our trainers are always accessible to you through our iOS and Android apps, where you can track your progress, schedule workouts, view goals, and integrate with other fitness apps like MyFitnessPal and FitBit.

Nutrition

From nutrition blogs to grocery shopping videos, with DevLifts Basic, you'll have access to content updated weekly.

Or go Pro and work directly with your online personal trainer to build a nutrition plan that's sustainable, effective, and tailored specifically to meet your individual needs. And with integrations like MyFitnessPal, your trainer will be able to check up with you and adjust your nutrition plan in real time. As always, you decide how involved you want you trainer to be.
